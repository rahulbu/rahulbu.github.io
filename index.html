01About

The model is the easy part.

I spent nearly five years building backend and search systems at Practo — from the search-indexer and ML learning-to-rank ranking, to India's ABDM national health-data integration, to the backend for a new secondary-care surgeries vertical — then moved into Generative AI. Now at Tellius I build our GenAI analytics assistant: agentic, multi-agent systems that turn plain English into real analysis. What I keep relearning is that whether an AI feature ships has less to do with the model and more to do with the engineering around it — retrieval, query translation, latency, caching, and knowing when not to use an LLM. The search and distributed-systems instincts I built pre-AI turn out to be the real edge.

B.E. Computer Science, Visvesvaraya Technological University (RNSIT) — 8.3 CGPA · Bengaluru, India
